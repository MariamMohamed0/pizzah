About Us

Ingredients

The main ingredients in pizza are a basic dough, tomato sauce, and cheese. The dough is typically made from flour, water, yeast, and salt. The tomato sauce often includes tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and herbs. Cheese, primarily mozzarella, adds a creamy texture and rich flavor. Additional toppings vary widely and can include meats, vegetables, and even fruits.
